The Red Coconut Club® is Orlando’s premier nightclub and ultra-lounge. It sits atop CityWalk®, where you can take in the stars on the balcony, mellow out at intimate tables inside, or mix with lively crowds around the bars. The couldn't-be-hipper décor is a little bit 1950s Vegas, a little bit Cuban, and a little bit Polynesian-tiki retro with vintage couches and bongo-shaped bar stools. You'll find three different bars spread across the club's two levels, featuring signature martinis and specialty drinks, an extensive wine list and VIP table service.
